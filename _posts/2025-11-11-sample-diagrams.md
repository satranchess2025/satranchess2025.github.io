# Diagrams

FEN to GIF chess diagram generator by Lichess works with the below Markdown syntax:

```
![image](https://lichess1.org/export/fen.gif?fen=[FEN])
```

Replace "[FEN]" with a valid FEN and the result will be a diagram:
