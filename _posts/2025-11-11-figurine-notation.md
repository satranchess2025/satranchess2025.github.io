A script crawls the page for chess notation and displays it in figurine.

For example, nbd2 will not render as a chess move, but Nbd2 will.

Here is a sample game from a raw PGN:

**Gramb, Marius (2142) - GM Bogdan-Daniel Deac (2690)**

**Reykjavik Open 2024**

1\. e4 c5 2. c3 d5 3. exd5 Qxd5 4. d4 Nf6 5. Nf3 e6 6. Na3 Qd8 7. Nc4 Be7 8. Nce5 O-O 9. Be2 cxd4 10. Qxd4 Qxd4 11. Nxd4 Nd5 12. O-O f6 13. Nef3 Rd8 14. c4 Nc7 15. Be3 e5 16. Nc2 Bf5 17. Rac1 Nba6 18. a3 Nc5 19. Bxc5 Bxc5 20. b4 Be7 21. Rfd1 Rxd1+ 22. Bxd1 a5 23. Ne3 Be6 24. Rb1 axb4 25. axb4 b6 26. Bc2 g6 27. g4 Kg7 28. g5 f5 29. Nxe5 Bxg5 30. c5 bxc5 31. bxc5 Ra5 32. Nd3 Nb5 33. f4 Bf6 34. Ne5 Bxe5 35. fxe5 Nd4 36. Rb7+ Kh6 37. c6 Rxe5 38. Kf2 f4 39. Ng2 Re2+ 40. Kg1 Rxc2 0-1

Please remember to start the game notation with "1 \ ." (without spaces) to prevent a numbered list.
